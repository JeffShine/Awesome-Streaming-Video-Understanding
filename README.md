# Awesome Streaming Video Understanding [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

> A curated list of research papers, models, and resources on **streaming**, **online**, and **real-time** video understanding with Large Multimodal Models (LMMs).  
> Focus areas include: streaming perception, proactive QA, real-time memory, KV compression, and token-efficient long video modeling.
